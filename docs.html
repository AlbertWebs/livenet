Getting started – log in

  1. Open your website address in the browser (for example http://localhost:8000 or the address your administrator gave you).
  2. Add /login at the end of the address (e.g. http://localhost:8000/login) and press Enter.
  3. Enter your email and password, then click the button to log in.
  4. You will see the Dashboard with a menu on the left. Use that menu to go to any part of the site you want to edit.

Site Settings (logo, contact info, intro text)

Click Site Settings in the left menu. Here you can change:

  • Branding – Company name, logo picture, and small icon (favicon) for the browser tab.
  • Homepage stats – The three numbers under “What Our Customers Say” (e.g. 99.9%, 50K+, 24/7). You can change both the number and the label (e.g. “Uptime SLA”, “Happy Customers”).
  • Intro section – The “About Livenet Solutions” text that appears below the top banner. Type or paste your text; use line breaks for new paragraphs.
  • Homepage images – Upload or replace the images used on the homepage (Internet Services image, decorative images). You can also remove an image; the site will show a placeholder.
  • Contact – Email (where contact and “Apply for Connection” messages go), phone number, and address. These appear in the footer and on the Contact page.
  • Social links – Full web addresses to your Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn pages.
  • SEO & link preview – Title and short description for search engines and when someone shares your homepage link.
  • Contact page map – If you use a Google Map on the Contact page, paste the embed link here.

Important: Scroll to the bottom and click Save settings when you are done. Nothing changes until you save.

Home Sections (main block on the homepage)

Click Home Sections in the left menu. You will see a list of sections. The one that controls the main “Internet Services” block on the homepage is named features.

  1. Find the row that says features and click Edit.
  2. Change the Title (e.g. “Internet Services for Home & Business”), the Subtitle (the short line under the title), and upload an Image if you want a picture next to the two service cards.
  3. Make sure Active is checked so the section is visible.
  4. Click the button to save. Your changes will appear in that block on the homepage.

If there is no “features” section yet, click Add section, set the name to features, then fill in the title, subtitle, and image as above.

Internet Plans (Home & Business packages)

Click Home Internet or Business Internet in the left menu to manage your packages. What you add or edit here appears on the homepage under “Internet Packages” and on the “Home Internet” or “Business Internet” page.

Adding a new plan

  1. Open Home Internet or Business Internet (depending on the type of plan).
  2. Click Add Home plan or Add Business plan.
  3. Fill in: plan name, speed (e.g. “50 Mbps”), price, currency (e.g. KES).
  4. In Features, type one feature per line (e.g. “Unlimited data”, “24/7 support”).
  5. Optionally add a badge (e.g. “Most Popular”), tick “Highlighted” to make it stand out, and upload a plan image.
  6. Set Sort order (lower numbers show first).
  7. Click the button to create the plan.

Editing or deleting a plan

From the list of plans, click Edit on a plan to change it, or delete it (you will be asked to confirm).

Articles (blog posts)

Click Articles in the left menu. Articles are the posts that appear on the “Articles” page of your website. Visitors can search and filter them by category.

  1. Click Add article.
  2. Enter a title and choose a category (e.g. Home Internet, Business, Tips).
  3. Write or paste your content in the editor. You can format text (bold, lists, etc.) using the toolbar.
  4. Optionally add a featured image and set the status to Published (so it appears on the site) or Draft (so only you see it).
  5. Save. To change or remove the image later, open the article and use “Remove image” if needed.

Other sections (Coverage, Applications, Media)

  • Coverage – Add or edit the list of areas you serve and the map on the “Our Coverage” page. Use the map tool to draw or adjust the coverage areas.
  • Application Requests – View everyone who submitted the “Apply for Connection” form. You can see their name, email, phone, and message. The contact email in Site Settings is where notification emails are sent.
  • Media Manager – Upload images or files here to use elsewhere (e.g. in plans, articles, or settings). You can also delete files you no longer need.
  • Pages – Create or edit custom pages (for future or extra pages on the site).
  • Testimonials – Add customer quotes; these can be used in future updates of the site.
